GitHub User Information Awareness Survey

Survey Introduction

Dear Participant(s), Thank you for participating in our study. In this study, we will ask to take a survey that should around 5 to 10 mins to complete. In this survey, we will ask you about your past public commits and meta-information associated with those commits. At the end of this study, you may choose to enter an email address for a chance to get a $50 Amazon eGift Card. Please see the Reward Statement for details. All data collected will adhere to our Privacy Statement. If you have any questions during or after the study, please contact Anda Liang via emails: anda.liang@vanderbilt.edu. You are free to withdraw from the study anytime you want. Thank you again for your participation!
